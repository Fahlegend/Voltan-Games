About Us

Voltan Games (VG) is a small independent brand created to bring all of our creative projects together under one name.

Founded in 2023 by Faheem and Jesse, Voltan Games began as a passion project during the start of our game development journey.

Our Future Goals

Beginning in 2026, our goal is to build a complete professional website showcasing everything we have created.

We aim to expand our collection of games while also offering high-quality 3D printed products ranging from useful everyday items to completely custom creations.

Our long-term vision is to build a recognizable and trusted brand known for creativity, quality, and innovation.
